AboutIPTC

↗ Founded in  1997

Founded in 2017 by Mehdi Salmanpour, the International Professional Training Certification (IPTC) is a non-profit organization that has evolved significantly over the years. Initially known by various names, it is now widely recognized as IPTC. The organization is endorsed by esteemed institutions such as the New York Chamber of Commerce, UAE Sports Council, Turkish Chamber of Commerce, various National Olympic Committees, Ministries of Sports, International Federations, Universities, and Sports Clubs.

International Professional Training Certification

IPTC is registered as an official non-profit organization in New York and as a for-profit organization in Dubai, UAE, and Istanbul, Turkey. Founded in 1997, IPTC is recognized today by the New York Chamber of Commerce, the UAE Sports Government, the Turkish Chamber of Commerce, various National Olympic Committees, Sports Ministries, international federations, international organizations, universities, sports clubs, and more. IPTC aims to become a unique international university capable of transforming the management level of sports federations worldwide.
